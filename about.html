About Our Fall Festival

Welcome to our annual, Fables Fall Festival, a cherished tradition in our community that celebrates the beauty and magic of autumn. Nestled in the heart of our charming Fables township, our festival is a joyous occasion that brings people together to revel in the splendor of the season.

Our Story

Fables Fall Festival began as a small gathering of friends and neighbors, a way to honor the harvest and the changing leaves. Over the years, it has grown into a beloved event that draws people from far and wide. What started as a humble celebration has blossomed into a grand spectacle, thanks to the unwavering support of our tight-knit community of Fables, Meridia.

Activities for All Ages

Our Fall Festival is designed to offer something for everyone. Families can enjoy hayrides, a petting zoo, and pumpkin decorating, while thrill-seekers can take on our corn maze and haunted house. For those who prefer a more relaxed pace, there's live music, local artisans, and delectable food vendors.

Farm-to-Table Delights

We take pride in showcasing the bounties of the season with an array of farm-fresh foods. Whether you're craving a warm slice of apple pie, a hearty bowl of butternut squash soup, or freshly roasted corn on the cob, our festival is a culinary delight.

Join Us!

We invite you to join us this fall for a day of joy, laughter, and the simple pleasures of the season. Whether you come for the breathtaking scenery, the mouthwatering treats, or simply to share a warm cup of apple cider with friends, our Fall Festival promises an unforgettable experience.
